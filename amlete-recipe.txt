Ingredients: 
 - 2 eggs
 - 50g of becon
 - 50g of broccoli
 - 50g of cheese
 - 200g of milk

Directions:
 1. Break 2 eggs into medium vessel with milk. 
 2. Mix energetically until homogeneous state.
 3. Slice becon and broccoli on small peaces and put them on hot saucepan.
 4. Fry them sterring for 3 minutes.
 5. Pour eggs with milk on the saucepan and fry for 6 minutes.
 6. Grate cheese on top of the amelete 1 minut prior the ready state.
